About Us

Our Animal Research and Rescue Center is a non-profit organization which provides a hands on and interative opprutunity for the youth to truly see and learn how we do research and resuce at risk animals in our enviroment. We are located in New York City, NY.

Our organization, Aniaml Reserach and Rescue Center is associated with Wildlife Rescue and Rehabilitation .

We help many animals, such as

  • Loggerhaed Sea Turtles

  • Red Wolves

  • Golden Eagles

  • River Herrings

  • Horseshoe Crabs

  • American Eels

  • Diamondback Terrapins

  • Piping Plovers

  • Atlantic Coast Leopard Frogs

  • Short-eared Owls

  • Black-footed Ferrets

  • Queen Snakes

  • Franklin's Bumblebees

This is Sammy the Sea Turtle which we found in Jones Beach State Park. She was flipped upside down and was struggling to make her way to the beach. With the help of our faculty and volunteers were able to bring her to shore safely. For more of our missions visit our Gallery.
