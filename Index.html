Welcome to our Animal Research and Rescue Center. Our goal is to teach and provide information to our youth about the various endangered animals and ways we can help them. We are a non-profit organization soley focused on teaching the youth and providing care for at risk animals.

Our staff members provide 24/7 care and assistance for whichever at risk animals we have at the facility. We also provide information to any of those which decide to meet us at our facility or contact us via email or call.
